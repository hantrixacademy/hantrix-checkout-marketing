About The Program

Hantrix Academy is a live mentorship program designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs launch and scale international Shopify stores from India. Through a proven step-by-step curriculum, you'll learn product research, store building, paid advertising, international payment setups, and the strategies needed to get your first sale.

Unlike traditional courses, Hantrix includes daily live mentoring calls, where you can ask questions, get expert feedback, and solve challenges in real time. Combined with hands-on implementation, recorded lessons, and community support, you'll have everything needed to build and grow a successful eCommerce business.
