Privacy Policy

Effective Date : May 5, 2026
Hantrix Academy values your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you register or purchase a course, we may collect:

Personal Information

Full name

Email address

Phone number

Payment-related details

Account login information

Technical Information

Device information

Browser type

IP address

Platform usage activity

02.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide course access

Deliver login credentials

Process payments

Offer customer support

Improve platform performance

Send important announcements

Communicate course updates

Payment Security

Hantrix Academy does not directly store sensitive payment card details.

Payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user information from:

Unauthorized access

Data misuse

Disclosure

Loss

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties.

Information may only be shared when:

Required by law

Necessary for payment processing

Required for platform functionality

07.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to:

Improve user experience

Track platform performance

Understand user behavior

Users may disable cookies through browser settings.

08.

Student Content

Content shared by students inside community sections (wins, questions, discussions) may be visible to other enrolled students.

Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information publicly.

09.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

Changes become effective immediately upon publication.

10.

Contact Us

For privacy-related concerns, students may contact Hantrix Academy through the platform support section.
